Do you ever wanted to make your own edits in the game? Did you ever feel that a feature could be improved? So it's time for modding!

In this guide we'll go through the process of how to develop your own mod and publishing it on Gamebanana.

:::caution
This is a working-in-progress page. More info will be added in the future.
:::

## Getting Started
To begin your journey of creating mods, you'll first need to extract the needed files from your game. You can do this by following our [Dumping RomFS Guide](../../guides/dumping-romfs).
